# Code of conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short:

- Be respectful, inclusive, and assume good intent.
- Disagreement on technical decisions is fine and welcome — disagreement on people's worth is not.
- Harassment, slurs, and personal attacks are not tolerated, ever.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, email **conduct@yoursign.tech**. Reports are confidential.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, temporarily ban, or permanently ban contributors for violations, at their discretion. We aim to communicate decisions clearly.
